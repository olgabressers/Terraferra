6.Assumptions to verify with Maxim before finalizing

  • Exchange rate at signing. 1.10 is a working figure. Rate locks at the signing date, not today.
  • Gross vs net salary. Is €8K gross (founder pays own NL/US tax) or net? Gross-up changes the cost line by 30–50%.
  • Maxim's $270K prior contribution. Is this counted toward the new $500K, or does the new $500K start clean?
  • CTO start date. Day 1, month 4, or month 6?
  • Office / co-working. Remote-first or co-working desks (~€500/mo per founder)?
  • Health insurance / pension. Founders cover personally from salary, or is this a corporate line item?
  • Board / observer compensation. Any cash or expense reimbursement for board attendance?
  • Currency of bank account. Delaware C-corp banks in USD. Salaries paid to NL contractors require FX. Bank/wire fees ~€20–50 per transfer × monthly = ~€2K/yr (rolled into contingency).
