5.Scenarios

Scenario USD raise EUR raise What changes / what it buys
A — Raise to plan $1,000,000 €909,091 Funds the stated team and full operating costs for 24 months with contingency. Doubles Maxim's stated target.
B — Hold $500K, accept ~12-month runway $500,000 €454,545 Covers stated team for ~12 months at full burn. Forces a follow-on raise inside year 1 — high pressure on revenue / traction.
C — Hold $500K, reduce contractor fees $500,000 €454,545 Founders at €5K/mo, CTO at €4.5K/mo. Saves ~€216K over 24 months. Brings total down to ~€709K = $780K. Still a ~$280K gap.
D — Hold $500K, deep cut $500,000 €454,545 All founders at €3K/mo. Saves ~€324K. Brings total down to ~€601K = $661K. Still a ~$161K gap. Below NL minimum-living for an experienced exec.
Bottom line
$500K does not buy 24 months of zero-revenue runway at the stated team. The realistic options are: (1) raise closer to $1.0M, or (2) raise $500K and accept a follow-on round inside 12 months. Any salary-cut scenario closes only part of the gap and still requires a follow-on.
