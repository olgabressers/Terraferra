03. Zero-budget acquisition channels — priority bullseye

P1 · Bullseye — compounding, zero cost
Designers bring their own audience. Every signed designer arrives with a 10K–150K-follower community. Target: pull 5% of those superfans onto Terra Ferra. Each new designer compounds the platform's audience at no marketing cost. (2023 founder interview archive.)
P2 · Active outbound — CEO-driven, zero ad spend
  • LTK / ShopMy excluded designers. Niche fashion creators with 10K–50K followers that LTK and ShopMy reject by follower-minimum policy.
  • Wolf & Badger + NOTHS sellers. ~7,000 designers paying 25–52% commission across both. Pitch: 7% take rate plus pre-order and co-creation tools.
  • Ablo designers. ~3,000 designers in a closed marketplace with no community, no pre-order, no demand validation before production.
  • Public LTK brand directory + Instagram as cold-outreach source lists.
P3 · Earned media + events — non-paid distribution
  • PR launch (Month 4) — coverage in 3+ industry titles within launch month.
  • Conference presence — NRF (January), Shoptalk (March), 4 trips per year.
  • EU geographic wedge — no competitor in the matrix actively recruits EU emerging designers. CEO based in NL is an operational advantage.

07. Risks & mitigations

RiskMitigation
ShopMy adds pre-order or co-creation to Circles ShopMy raised $147.5M and grew 200% YoY. Build EU emerging-designer supply faster than ShopMy can extend Circles eastward. EU recruitment is Year-1 priority.
Pre-orders don't convert at scale Services-led model means each designer is a managed engagement, not a self-serve listing. Designer success is the deliverable; failed drops trigger CEO intervention, not silent churn.
Designer churn at $1,000/month after Month 3 prepay Retention KPI: churn < 10%. Designer NPS tracked monthly. Pricing structured as 3 months prepaid ensures revenue floor and gives 90 days to prove ROI.
$500K bridge does not close by Month 3 Founder runs investor pipeline continuously, not as a single round. Rolling SAFE structure permits closes at any point. CEO unpaid initially to extend runway.
TikTok Shop adds fashion-specific designer recruitment TikTok Shop is mass-market and seller-base-heavy; TF's defence is curated emerging-designer focus, niche-influencer inclusion, and pre-launch demand formation that TikTok Shop does not address.
