Questions

How it all works.

What is SMTHG?

A platform for limited fashion drops from independent designers. Creators style collections first. You discover through creators, try on virtually, and pre-order before the window closes.

Do I pay before the item is made?

Yes — that's the model. You place your order during the drop window. Once orders are confirmed, the designer produces exactly what was sold. Each designer sets their own production timeline.

How does Virtual Try-On work?

Upload a photo, select a piece, and see it on your own body. The AI Stylist adds outfit recommendations. Far fewer returns — you already know what it looks like on you.

Who are the designers?

Independent emerging labels in premium and affordable-luxury — niche, with a distinct visual identity. Designers who want demand confirmed before they produce a single item.

What if the drop sells out before I get there?

Subscribe and get early access 24 hours before every drop goes public. Subscribers always get first access to the limited quantity.

Is this better for the planet?

Pre-orders eliminate overproduction by design — nothing gets manufactured without confirmed demand. Zero surplus means zero destruction.
