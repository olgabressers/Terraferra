Terra Ferra — Roles & Scope

Scenario basis. All figures and timing on this page follow the Base scenario of the Terra Ferra Operating Budget: 10 paying designers and $10K MRR by Month 6. Product, legal and launch are sequenced so designer onboarding can begin at Month 3, leaving the runway needed to reach 10 paying designers by Month 6. The Optimistic scenario reaches each milestone sooner; the Pessimistic scenario later.
