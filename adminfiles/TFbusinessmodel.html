Where the gap sits

  • Pre-production entry. TF is the only platform that signs designers with sketches and zero inventory. Every other platform — ShopMy, LTK, Ablo, W&B, NOTHS — requires existing inventory to participate.
  • Pre-order + crowdfunding for fashion. No competitor in this matrix offers either function for fashion designers.
  • Co-creation. Community input into design is unique to TF across this matrix.
  • Superfan / ambassador earnings layer. Unique to TF; LTK and ShopMy run only traditional influencer affiliate networks.
  • 10K–150K niche fashion influencers. LTK and ShopMy exclude this segment by follower-minimum policy; TF has no minimum.
  • EU designer / influencer recruitment. No platform in this matrix actively recruits EU emerging designers or EU niche influencers; LTK is Germany-only and mass-market.
  • What TF does not have. AI design generation (Ablo's territory); platform-owned audience at scale (LTK, W&B, NOTHS); live track record (TF is pre-launch in this restart).
  • Closest competitive threat. ShopMy — 200% YoY, profitable, $147.5M raised, Circles community launched July 2025. Capital advantage and an early community move shorten TF's window.
