Why pre-order matters
Fashion makes too much.
Most of it is destroyed.

The fashion industry produces 30–40% more than it sells. Unsold inventory gets incinerated or sent to landfill — not donated, not recycled.

Pre-orders change the logic entirely: nothing gets made until it's sold. Every piece has a buyer before production starts. Zero waste by design.

Something to tell you

Questions

What is SMTHG?

A platform for limited fashion drops from independent designers. Creators style collections first. Consumers discover through creators, try on virtually, and pre-order before the window closes. Production only happens after demand is confirmed.

Who are the designers on SMTHG?

Independent emerging labels in the premium and affordable-luxury segment — niche, with a distinct visual identity and a growing community. Designers who want to test demand before committing to production runs.

Do I pay before the item is made?

Yes — that's the model. You pre-order during the drop window. Once orders are confirmed, the designer produces exactly what was sold. Drop windows and production timelines are set clearly by each designer.

How does Virtual Try-On work?

Upload a photo and see how any piece looks on your own body before purchasing. The AI Stylist adds outfit recommendations. No fitting rooms, no guesswork — and significantly fewer returns.

Why is this better for the planet?

Fashion overproduces by 30–40% — and the surplus is largely destroyed, not donated. Pre-orders eliminate that: nothing gets made without confirmed demand. SMTHG is sustainable by design, not as a marketing label.

How do I join as a founding designer?

SMTHG is selecting its first group of designers now. Founding designers receive dedicated onboarding, priority placement in the first drops, and a direct line to the platform team. Leave your details below.
