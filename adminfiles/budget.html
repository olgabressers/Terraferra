6.Operating Assumptions

  • All compensation is gross. Each founder invoices the corporation as an independent freelance contractor.
  • All three founders commence services Day 1.
  • Health insurance and pension: personal expense of each contractor, excluded from corporate budget.
  • Board attendance and investor representation expenses: included within T&E; no separate line.
  • FX rate: 1 EUR = 1.10 USD, 3-month average (Feb–May 2026). ECB reference rate to be confirmed at signing.
  • Budget commences from zero. Prior personal contributions are not counted toward this plan.
  • Revenue assumed at zero across both years for runway calculation purposes.

Revenue on the services-led model from the 2026 strategic deck — managed launch sprints at $1,000/month plus pre-order processing fees. All figures USD.
Earning Formula — Services-Led Model
Total Revenue = $1,000/month service subscription  +  5% × pre-orders processed  +  7% × co-creator rewards above $10,000/month
  • $1,000/month service subscription — managed drop-launch sprint package, per paying designer (billed three months upfront).
  • 5% × pre-orders processed — fee on pre-order and gift-card volume run through the platform.
  • 7% × co-creator rewards above $10,000/month — applies only to designers whose co-creator/collaborator reward payouts exceed $10,000/month; incremental, not modelled in the core scenarios below.
Source: Terra Ferra pricing page and 2026 positioning & GTM strategic deck.
