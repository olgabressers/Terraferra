04 MVP Scope
What's included at launch
  • Pre-order drops — designer lists item, sets quantity and drop window
  • Influencer referral links — unique tracked link per influencer per drop
  • Virtual try-on (VTO) — AI image-to-image rendering, no 3D models needed
  • Early-buyer badge — awarded to first N orders per drop
  • Points per order — redeemable for future discount (basic loyalty)
  • Designer dashboard — orders, revenue, per-influencer performance
  • Payout split — auto at checkout: designer / TF fee / influencer share
Added in a later version
  • Co-creation (community votes on designs)
  • AI influencer matching
  • Social feed and prosumer upload layer
  • Full gamification suite — tiers, quests, tokenization
  • Consumer-to-consumer marketplace
  • B2B / brand direct integration — TF acting as white-label infrastructure for established fashion brands to run their own drops and campaigns directly (different use case from serving emerging designers)

06 Gamification
What ships with the pre-order launch
MVP · Fastest to ship
Invite-to-Win

Turn each pre-order into a referral loop. After ordering, the buyer creates their own VTO of the product and invites friends via Instagram, Facebook, or TF. Every friend who joins TF and places the pre-order earns the inviter a reward. Both sides receive an incentive — the friend gets a welcome reward too.

How it works
  1. Pre-order goes live; influencer publishes it with VTO.
  2. Buyer creates their own VTO of the product inside TF.
  3. They share it to Instagram, Facebook, or a TF referral link.
  4. Friend clicks, joins TF, and places the pre-order.
  5. Inviter earns a reward from the ladder below.
Example reward ladder
Completed invitesInviter earns
1 friendFree shipping + 150 pts
3 friendsStore credit (~$15 equivalent)
5 friendsMystery gift / sample
10 friendsVIP early access to next drop
A "successful invite" counts only when the friend creates an account and places the pre-order. Self-referrals are blocked by payment method and device fingerprint. Reward is reversed if the friend's order is cancelled. Rewardable invites are capped per campaign to protect margin. Points are held until the pre-order clears its cancellation window.
Source: TerraFerra_Gamification.pdf, p.7–9
MVP
Early Buyer Badge

Awarded to the first N orders on each drop. Permanent visible badge on the buyer's TF profile. Creates urgency and scarcity around each drop window — buyers who move fast get a status marker no one else can earn after the drop closes.

MVP · Meta-layer
Points & Tiers

Points earned on every order, redeemable for future discounts. The unified meta-layer that connects all three gamification mechanics — Invite-to-Win, Co-Design and Style Battle all feed the same point balance. Build it once in MVP; Phase 2 mechanics plug into the same system without a rebuild.

Source: TerraFerra_Gamification.pdf, p.12
Phase 2 — requires co-creation flow or full product catalog
Phase 2
Co-Design

Designer publishes 3 versions of a product. The community votes and predicts which one wins — time-boxed campaign. Each version has its own VTO, shareable only inside TF (protects unreleased designs from leaking). When the countdown ends, the designer picks the winning version; it moves into the pre-order flow.

Everyone who predicted correctly wins a prize. TF covers a base reward (store credit or points) for every correct guesser. The designer optionally adds a premium "money-can't-buy" prize — a signed piece, exclusive colorway, or 1-of-1 item — for the top winners.

Source: TerraFerra_Gamification.pdf, p.4–6
Phase 2 · TF Recommendation
Style Battle

Weekly UGC styling contest. A featured pre-order item gets a theme (e.g. "Golden Hour"). Users style a look around it using real items from the TF catalog and submit to a public gallery — every item in every look is shoppable.

Community votes on a time-boxed leaderboard. Top looks are featured on TF; entrants earn points and voters get a discount. Reuses the voting engine from Co-Design — low additional build cost. UGC creates organic reach and SEO traffic. Shoppable looks convert directly.

Source: TerraFerra_Gamification.pdf, p.10–11
