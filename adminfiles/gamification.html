3
Super fan
Video made — two paths
Path A · AI-generated. Super fan picks a piece, uploads one selfie. AI generates a video of them in the piece. No purchase needed.

Path B · Raw amateur UGC. Super fan films themselves wearing the actual piece. Higher authenticity but requires they own / received a sample of the drop.

Style name (both paths). The super fan gives the look a name (e.g. "quiet-luxury Tokyo"). Style names accumulate as a discovery tag library across drops.
Tools — Path A
HiggsfieldRec
HeyGen
PixVerse
Money
TF paysPath A: per-video AI API fee. Path B: zero AI cost.
CostSuper fan: time + one selfie (Path A) or self-filmed clip (Path B).
Risk · unit economics TF must break even per video: AI generation fee + post bonus + view bonus < 7% GMV commission earned on attributed pre-orders. If the video does not drive enough pre-orders, TF loses money on it. Set a cost ceiling per drop, and pause videos from super fans whose past videos failed to convert.
4
Designer + Auto
Approved + cross-posted
Designer reviews the video before it goes live under their tag — approve, reject, or request edits. 24h SLA. If no response, the TF curator can approve as fallback. Approved videos auto-post to TF, Instagram and TikTok with the designer tag and the pre-order link.
Channels
TF feed
Instagram
TikTok
Money
CostDesigner time for review (per video). If they don't review, TF curator time as fallback.
TF paysCross-post API calls. Negligible.
Risk · approval + platform policy Three issues. (1) Approval bottleneck: a slow designer kills the loop. Enforce a 24h SLA; default to TF-curator fallback approval after that. (2) API access: Instagram / TikTok auto-post APIs work only for Business / Creator accounts; personal accounts share manually via OS share sheet. (3) AI-content disclosure: TikTok and Instagram both require AI-generated content to be labelled. TF must add the flag on every Path-A cross-post, or the video can be down-ranked or removed.

TF settles
Bonus paid · pre-orders settled
Super fan earns: fixed post bonus + capped view bonus + commission on pre-orders driven by their video.

Money
Consumer paysPre-order amount → TF (full payment).
TF earns7% GMV commission on pre-orders + 10% take on the designer-paid super-fan commission (existing TF model). The 10% applies only to designer-paid commissions, not to TF-paid bonuses.
Designer paysSuper-fan commission % on pre-orders driven by the video. Settled via TF.
TF paysFixed post bonus + capped view bonus (out of TF margin) → Super fan.
Risk · fraud A super fan could pay for fake views — bot farms are services that generate clicks from fake accounts — and collect the view bonus without doing any real promotion. TF needs three guards: verify views come from real users, cap how much one super fan can earn per drop, and cap total view-bonus payout per drop and per day.
Build investment · one-time
What TF builds upfront
  • Higgsfield (or HeyGen / PixVerse) API integration
  • Theme curation tool for TF curators
  • KPI tracking — posts, views, pre-order attribution
  • View-source verification + fraud guards
  • Cross-post pipeline to TF / Instagram / TikTok
Operating cost · per drop
What TF spends per drop
  • AI video API fees (per video generated)
  • Fixed post bonus per super fan (out of TF margin)
  • Capped view bonus per super fan (out of TF margin)
  • Curator time (theme + moderation)
Revenue · per drop
What TF earns per drop
  • 7% GMV commission on pre-orders (existing TF model)
  • 10% take on designer-paid super-fan commissions only (existing 2022 TF model)
  • Cross-posted videos act as discovery content for the next drop
  • Specific cost ranges — engineering days, per-video AI fee, break-even pre-order conversion — to be set with the CTO before launch

★★★
Creators post product videos; commission per sale tracked through affiliate links. Tier-based bonus structure: higher monthly sales unlock higher commission tiers + promo periods set by brand.
Sales-tied commissionTier bonusBrand-fundedTikTok-locked
PricingBrand-funded commissions; rates set per brand
Revenue modelTikTok takes a platform fee on every TikTok Shop transaction; creators take commission set by brand
Target audienceBrands selling on TikTok Shop + creators with TikTok presence meeting eligibility
IntegrationTikTok-internal program (cannot be re-used by TF)
Bonus type% commission per sale + tier bonus on volume
Reward KPIEach tracked sale through the affiliate link → commission paid. Monthly sales volume crosses brand-set threshold → tier bonus.
ClientsAny TikTok Shop seller
GeographyMarkets where TikTok Shop operates
Relevance to TFReference only — not integrable. Useful as a benchmark for the tier-based bonus structure TF could adopt for super fans (e.g. higher commission unlocked at higher monthly volume).
