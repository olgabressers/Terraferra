Questions

What brands ask us.

What does it cost to list on SMTHG?

Listing is free. SMTHG charges 7% on GMV when a drop sells. You pay only on confirmed sales — no monthly fees, no listing fees, no pay-per-click.

Do I have to be based in the US?

No. SMTHG serves the US consumer market. Founding designer partners are welcome from the US, EU, and UK. Your consumers buy through the US platform regardless of where you're based.

How much control do I have over my brand presentation?

Complete control. You supply your own campaign imagery, lookbook, and brand copy. SMTHG does not apply standardised marketplace styling to your listings. What makes your label distinctive stays visible.

How does the creator layer work?

SMTHG matches your drop with creators whose aesthetic and audience fits your brand. They get early access 24 hours before the public window, create their own content, and share it. You pay a commission on sales they generate — not on reach or impressions.

What's the minimum production run?

There is no minimum. You set the quantity. Some designers run drops of 20 pieces; others run 200. What gets ordered is what gets produced — you decide the ceiling, not the floor.
