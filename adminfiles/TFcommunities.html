Fashion Communities by Audience
Existing communities and programs serving designers, creators, shoppers, brand fans, and ambassadors — with what a single cross-designer platform can offer each group that single-brand and single-purpose platforms cannot.
SMTHG Platform — Relevant to Every Group
Audience What existing communities are built around SMTHG platform
Designers / Brands Trade associations (CFDA, BFC NEWGEN) and marketplaces (Garmentory) — membership fees, election criteria, or a marketplace cut, with no built-in ambassador layer. Commission-based entry (7% GMV), no membership fee, pre-order drops, built-in ambassador revenue share.
Influencers / Creators Creator marketplaces (Aspire, Fohr, GRIN) match brands and creators through applications, self-serve search, or paid brand-side platform fees. AI designer-creator matching per drop; commission from every designer on the platform, not a subset.
Consumers / Shoppers Resale marketplaces (Depop, Poshmark, Vinted, StockX) trade secondhand and resale inventory, not new emerging-designer drops. Pre-order access to emerging-designer drops, with virtual try-on before purchase.
Fans / Loyalty Loyalty programs (Nike Membership, Levi's Red Tab, Vans Family, Foot Locker FLX) are single-brand, points-and-perks only. Community mechanics — challenges, leaderboards, badges — spanning every designer on the platform, not one brand.
Ambassador Programs Ambassador programs (REVOLVE, Aerie Realmakers, PrettyLittleThing, Princess Polly) are single-retailer, each with its own follower gate and commission rate. Every designer on the platform commissionable by every ambassador from day one, on a standardized revenue share.
