Fashion Communities by Audience
Real fashion communities hosted on general community platforms — Skool, Circle, Mighty Networks, Discord — with what a shared, commerce-connected community can offer that a standalone paid community cannot.
SMTHG Platform — What's Missing on These Platforms
Platform type What it's built around SMTHG platform
Paid course/community software (Skool) Small, fragmented communities (1–125 members found), free to $99/month, no built-in commerce or ambassador payouts. Community tied directly to shoppable designer drops, with ambassador revenue share built in.
Brand-hosted community software (Circle, Mighty Networks) Each brand builds and hosts its own separate community; fashion presence on both is thin. One shared cross-designer community — no per-brand build required.
Discord Large organic fan communities (one found at ~31,800 members) with no native commerce or ambassador structure. Same scale of community engagement, plus native pre-order and ambassador commission.
