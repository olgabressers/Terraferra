smthg. Operations Team

Job scope, hire triggers, US market salaries, and headcount roadmap from MVP to Year 5. Customer service scoped for one English-language shift, US opening hours (9am–6pm ET).

Role Definitions & Hire Triggers

Customer Service
English · 1 shift · 9am–6pm ET
  • Resolve buyer tickets: orders, tracking, returns, payment disputes via Intercom
  • Handle designer-side issues: dashboard access, drop submission errors, payout queries
  • Maintain SLA: <4hr first response, <24hr resolution; CSAT tracking per brand
  • Build and maintain macros and FAQ to deflect recurring queries
  • Flag quality complaints and fraud patterns to ops lead
Hire when: 150 tickets/month (first part-time). Full-time at 400/month. +1 rep per 500 additional tickets/month.
Capacity: 25–35 tickets/agent/day · Source: Jitbit 1,000-company study
$3,200–4,300/mo $38–52K/yr Source: BLS SOC 43-1011 · US median $38.7K
Content Creation
Social · Drop assets · Email
  • Produce drop announcement assets: images, reels, captions for designer launches
  • Manage SMTHG social calendar (Instagram, TikTok) — 5–7 posts/week
  • Build per-designer content kits for onboarding (brand templates, tone guidelines)
  • Brief AI image tools (Midjourney / Canva) and review for brand consistency
  • Own email and push templates for drop countdowns and new collection alerts
Hire when: 5 active brands (first contractor). Full-time FTE at 10 brands or 8+ drops/month.
$3,800–5,500/mo $46–66K/yr Source: Glassdoor/LinkedIn Salary 2025 · Social Media / Content Manager, fashion, US
Community Manager
Designers · Influencers · So-Me-Space
  • Onboard pilot designers: profile setup, first drop walkthrough, rules briefing
  • Curate and moderate So-Me-Space feed — quality standards, spam, tribal curation
  • Activate influencer relationships: drop briefings, commission reporting, UGC coordination
  • Run monthly designer success reviews: sell-through rates, engagement, recommendations
  • Build community rituals: style tribe features, drop countdowns, new designer spotlights
Hire when: 3 active designers (from day one of pilots). +1 at 20 active community members needing curation. Add influencer-specific role at 50+ active influencers.
$4,000–6,200/mo $48–74K/yr Source: Glassdoor/LinkedIn Salary 2025 · Community Manager, e-commerce, US
Platform Developers
Backend · Frontend · Mobile
  • Backend: API, GMV reporting, Stripe/VTO integrations, designer dashboards, security
  • Frontend/mobile: drop browsing, checkout flow, influencer commission views, app performance
  • Code reviews, dependency updates, on-call rotation (from Y3)
  • DevOps/infra added at $3M+ GMV (CI/CD, cloud cost optimisation, load testing)
  • Mobile specialist added at 100K+ MAU; senior full-stack at $5M+ GMV
Start: 2 contractors at MVP. +1 FTE per major feature track from Y2. Dedicated mobile engineer from Y3–Y4.
$9,200–13,300/mo $110–160K/yr · senior backend Source: BLS SOC 15-1252 · US median $130K · entry $80–100K

Year 1 budget uses offshore contractor rates — not US market rates. The 1st Year Budget page prices Community Manager and Customer Ops at $2,500/mo each — offshore contractor rates from lower-cost markets (Thailand, Netherlands, etc.). US market equivalents for those roles are $4,000–6,200/mo. The scaling table above uses US rates from Year 2 onward. Year 1 remains viable at contractor rates; switching to US-based hires at Y2 is a deliberate cost step-up tied to funding milestones.

Salary ranges are approximate US market figures. Customer Service: BLS SOC 43-1011, median $38.7K/yr (2023). Developers: BLS SOC 15-1252, median $130K/yr (2023). Content and Community salaries: Glassdoor / LinkedIn Salary, fashion/e-commerce, US, 2025 — approximate, verify before hiring. GMV targets are directional; actual staffing should be triggered by the per-role metrics above, not the revenue calendar.
