Fashion Brands Worldwide

Market size of independent fashion brand businesses across key geographies — and how many have been reached by existing platforms. Asian brands are covered separately as an emerging expansion pool.

Asian Fashion Brands — International Expansion

Asia is the world's largest fashion production base and an accelerating source of consumer-facing brands moving into EU and US markets. South Korea's apparel exports grew 45% in four years. Chinese designers are regularly featured at Paris and London Fashion Weeks. Southeast Asia's online fashion market grew 630% in a decade. These brands lack the community infrastructure and pre-order tooling to scale in Western markets — that is the gap TF addresses.

Brand Market Size vs. Platform Reach

Total independent fashion brands in the biggest markets vs. how many each comparable platform has onboarded. The gap is the untapped opportunity.
The US has 32,253 registered fashion design businesses (IBISWorld 2026) — independent labels, design studios, and freelance designers operating as businesses (NAICS 54149). Of those, 3,061 are already selling designer clothing online DTC (IBISWorld 2025). The EU has ~262,000 clothing retail stores (Eurostat via Statista, 2020) and ~123,510 clothing manufacturing companies — the actual producing brands (Eurostat via Statista, 2022). All comparable platforms combined have onboarded roughly 15,600 brands — a fraction of either market.

LTK (RewardStyle) 6,000+ Brand and retailer partners paying to access LTK's creator network. Established brands only; gated by follower minimums on creator side. LTK · 2024–25 LTK targets established mid-to-large brands. Emerging designers excluded by economic floor.

Where the gap sits

  • Pre-production entry. TF is the only platform that signs designers with sketches and zero inventory.
  • Pre-order + crowdfunding for fashion. No platform in this matrix offers either function for fashion designers.
  • Co-creation. Community input into design is unique to TF across this matrix.
  • Superfan / ambassador earnings layer. Unique to TF; LTK and ShopMy run only traditional influencer affiliate networks.
  • 10K–150K niche fashion influencers. LTK and ShopMy exclude this segment; Instagram, TikTok and Rednote include them but are not fashion-specific commerce platforms.
  • EU emerging-designer recruitment. No competitor in this matrix actively recruits EU emerging designers.
  • Asian brand infrastructure gap. K-fashion, Chinese designer, and SE Asian lifestyle brands moving into EU/US have no community platform built for pre-order launch and ambassador-led growth.
  • Closest direct competitive threat. ShopMy — 200% YoY, profitable, $147.5M raised, Circles community launched July 2025.
