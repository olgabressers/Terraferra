Why pre-order matters
Fashion makes too much.
Most of it is destroyed.

The fashion industry overproduces by 30–40%. Unsold inventory is incinerated or sent to landfill.

Pre-orders change the logic: nothing gets made until it's sold. Your purchase decides what gets produced — and nothing more.

Questions

How it all works.

What is smthg?

A platform for limited fashion drops from independent designers, built around a community you join before you buy. Discover and share, try on virtually, and pre-order before the window closes.

What is So-Me-Space?

A free, shared community for the whole platform. You join to discover and share — the feed includes drop announcements, style spaces, live sessions, and discussion with other members.

Do I pay before the item is made?

A deposit holds your spot during the drop window. The balance is charged only once orders are confirmed and the designer starts production. Each designer sets their own production timeline.

How does Virtual Try-On work?

Upload a photo, select a piece, and see it on your own body. The AI Stylist adds outfit recommendations. Far fewer returns — you already know what it looks like on you.

Who are the designers?

Independent emerging labels in premium and affordable-luxury — niche, with a distinct visual identity. Designers who want demand confirmed before they produce a single item.

Can I share drops with friends?

Yes — share your favorite items straight from the drop. It's how your Space grows with people who share your taste.

What if the drop sells out before I get there?

Subscribe and get notified 24 hours before every drop goes public — so you never miss it. So-Me-Space members always get first access to the limited quantity.

Is this better for the planet?

Pre-orders eliminate overproduction by design — nothing gets manufactured without confirmed demand. Zero surplus means zero destruction.
