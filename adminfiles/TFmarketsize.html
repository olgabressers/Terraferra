Note on the 2022 deck vs real 2026 data. The deck projected $64B for "global fashion influencer sales" in 2026 using 12.8% CAGR from a 2020 base. Real data shows: US social commerce alone reached $122.7B in 2026 (eMarketer), of which fashion is ~$34B US-only. The deck's $64B figure was likely intended as global — consistent with the real scale when Europe is added (~$34B US + ~$47B Europe (TF team estimate — no primary source) = ~$81B). The direction and magnitude were broadly correct; the construction issue is the spend/GMV conflation, not the headline number.
