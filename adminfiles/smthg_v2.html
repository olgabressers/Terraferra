Questions

What is SMTHG?

SMTHG is a fashion discovery platform connecting emerging designers, influencers, and consumers through limited drops and Virtual Try-On. Designers release intentional, small-run collections. Creators style and share them first. Consumers discover unique pieces, try them on virtually, share with their community, and buy before the drop sells out.

Who are the designers on SMTHG?

SMTHG is for independent emerging fashion brands in the premium and affordable-luxury segment — ready-to-wear, capsule collections, accessories, and art objects. Charismatic labels with their own visual identity and a growing community, not yet known beyond their city or country. SMTHG is the door to wider discovery.

Who is the core SMTHG consumer?

The core SMTHG consumer values uniqueness and individuality, chooses independent labels over global mass-luxury brands, and discovers fashion through creators they trust. They engage through visual content and move fashion through community — not advertising.

What is a limited drop?

A limited drop is a small, intentional collection with a defined window and quantity. Drops create genuine scarcity — pieces sell out on launch and become sought-after on resale, often trading at multiples of the original price. Production follows only confirmed demand: what gets ordered gets made.

How does Virtual Try-On work?

SMTHG's Virtual Try-On lets consumers and creators see how a garment looks on their own body before purchasing. Upload a photo, select a piece, and see yourself in it. The AI Stylist adds outfit-building recommendations based on your look. Discovery becomes personal — and returns from poor fit drop significantly.

How is SMTHG different from other fashion platforms?

Most fashion platforms are catalogues — endless product listings with no context and no community. SMTHG is built around a shared moment: the drop has a story, a window, and creators who bring it to life. Unlike Farfetch or Yoox, designers keep their own visual identity — their lookbooks, campaigns, brand universe. Virtual Try-On makes discovery personal. Pre-orders align production with real demand.

What kinds of creators can participate?

SMTHG is for any creator whose world intersects with fashion — style influencers, musicians, actors, athletes, architects, artists, street artists. The platform connects creators with drops that fit their aesthetic and audience, and rewards them when their community buys. Authentic recommendation, not sponsored placement.

How do creators earn on SMTHG?

Creators get early access to drops before they go wide. They use SMTHG's Virtual Try-On to create content and share it with their audience. When their community purchases through their recommendation, they earn a revenue share on the sale — directly rewarding authentic discovery.

Do consumers buy before items are produced?

On SMTHG, drops use a pre-order model: consumers secure pieces during the drop window, and production follows once demand is confirmed. What you order gets made for you — no leftover stock, no mass-market compromise. Drop windows and estimated production timelines are set clearly by each designer.

How does the superfan engagement work?

Superfans engage with their favourite brands through themed content challenges, style invention formats, and community moments — inventing new style directions, naming them, and sharing them on and off platform. Participation earns bonuses from the brands they love.

Which markets does SMTHG serve, and where can designers be based?

SMTHG launches in the US market — consumers discover and purchase through the US platform. Designers do not need to be US-based to participate: emerging designers from the EU and the UK are welcome as founding partners alongside US-based designers.

How do I join as a founding designer or creator?

SMTHG is in its founding phase, selecting its first group of designers and creator partners. Founding designers receive dedicated onboarding, early platform access, and priority placement in the first drops. Creators are matched with drops that fit their aesthetic.
